Job Market Paper

Inflation in Disaggregated Small Open Economies

This paper studies inflation in small open economies with production networks. I show that the production network alters the elasticity of the consumer price index (CPI) to changes in sectoral technology, factor prices, and import prices relative to closed economies (with or without production networks) and small open economies without production networks. This matters quantitatively and allows us to get closer to observed inflation in Chile and the United Kingdom during COVID-19.
