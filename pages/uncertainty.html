Economic Uncertainty

I've worked in the measurement of economic (policy) uncertainty (with Cerda and Valente) in Chile. In that journey, we constructed two indices using methods pioneered by Baker, Bloom and Davis (2016).

This index measures economic uncertainty and it is updated monthly by the Latin American Center for Economic and Social Policies (CLAPES), Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile. It can be accessed from here. If you want to take a deeper look into the index construction, here is a summary (in spanish). For an english version, see my paper with Cerda and Valente in the research section.
