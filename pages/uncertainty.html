Economic Uncertainty

I've worked in the measurement of economic (policy) uncertainty (with Cerda and Valente) in Chile. In that journey, we've constructed two indices using the methods pioneered by Baker, Bloom and Davis (2016).

The first index, which measure economic uncertainty, is updated monthly by the Latin American Center for Economic and Social Policies, Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile and can be accesed from here.

The second index, which measure economic policy uncertainty, is also updated monthly and is available here. That website is hosted by professors Baker, Bloom, and Davis (yes, the same of the seminal paper!). We are grateful to them for consider our index as a source for their larger database.
